For any $$x, y \in H$$, there exists a decomposition to the sum of vectors in $$S$$ and $$S^{\perp}$$: $$x = x_1 + x_2 , ~ y = y_1 + y_2$$, where $$x_1, y_1 \in S$$ and $$x_2, y_2 \in S^{\perp}$$. Then,

$$<Px, y> = <x_1, y_1 + y_2> = <x_1 , y_1> + <x_1, y_2> = <x_1, y_1>$$, and
$$<x, Py> = <x_1 + x_2, y_1> = <x_1, y_1> + <x_2, y_1> = <x_1, y_1>$$.

Thus we have $$<Px, y> = <x, Py>$$ for all $$x, y \in H$$, and therefore $$P = P^*$$.
