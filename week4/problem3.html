From the previous questions we now know that $$\mathrm{Range}(A)$$ and $$\ker A$$ are orthogonal and spans the space $$H$$.

Any $$x \in H$$ can be written as $$x = x_1 + x_2$$ where $$x_1 \in \ker A$$ and $$x_2 \in \overline{\mathrm{Range}(A)}$$.

Therefore, $$H = \ker A \bigoplus \overline{\mathrm{Range}(A)}$$.
